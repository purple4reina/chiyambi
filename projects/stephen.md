Stephen is a hardworking father of six in Katete, Zambia, working to make
the most of his land. Without an efficient irrigation system, he spent hours
each day manually watering his crops with a watering can — a slow,
exhausting process that limited how much he could grow.

### The Process

Once Stephen's project was fully funded, our team purchased a water pump and
delivered it directly to his farm.

### The Results

Watch below as Stephen sees his new water pump for the first time. With
reliable irrigation, Stephen can now water his entire plot, increase his
harvest, and share the benefit with neighboring farmers who work the same
land.
