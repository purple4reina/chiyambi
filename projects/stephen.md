Stephen is a hardworking father of six in Katete, Zambia, whose large plot
of land holds real promise — if only he had the means to properly irrigate
it. Without an efficient irrigation system, Stephen spent hours every single
day manually watering his vegetables with nothing but a watering can, a
labor-intensive routine that left him exhausted and unable to cultivate
more than a fraction of his land. That inefficiency didn't just limit his
harvest; it limited his ability to provide for his six children and to
contribute to the wider community around him.

### The Process

Our team saw an opportunity to turn Stephen's untapped land into a genuine
asset: a water pump that could irrigate his entire plot instead of the
small patches he could reach by hand. Once the project was fully funded,
our team purchased the pump and delivered it directly to his farm. The
moment we arrived, we witnessed pure, unfiltered joy from a man who had
spent countless hours each day battling the limits of a watering can — a
reaction that spoke volumes about just how much this simple piece of
equipment meant to him.

### The Results

Watch below as Stephen's eyes fill with tears of gratitude, realizing that
his backbreaking daily routine is about to change for good: no more
exhausted evenings, no more watching entire sections of his land sit
uncultivated for lack of water. With reliable irrigation, Stephen can now
water his whole plot, dramatically increase his harvest, and feed his six
children with abundance rather than scarcity — and because his land borders
his neighbors' gardens, the benefit of this pump will ripple out to the
other farmers who share the same water source, strengthening food security
for the wider community, not just his own household.
