Students in Katete, Zambia, faced a harsh reality: frequent power cuts left
them unable to study after dark, hurting their exam performance and
educational goals.

Thanks to the generosity of our supporters, solar lights were provided to
these dedicated learners, giving them a reliable, sustainable source of
light to study by — regardless of the grid's inconsistencies.
