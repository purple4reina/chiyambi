Students in Katete, Zambia, faced a harsh reality: frequent power cuts left
them unable to study after dark, hurting their exam performance and
educational goals.

### The Process

Once the project was fully funded, our team purchased solar lights for the
students of Katete. Along the way, the bus company transporting the lights
lost the shipment. Our team kept supporters updated on the setback and
worked to recover it before the lights could finally be delivered.

### The Results

Watch below as the community first celebrates the news, then hear directly
from a member of our team on the road to delivery, and finally see the
students receive their solar lights — giving them a reliable, sustainable
source of light to study by, regardless of the grid's inconsistencies.
