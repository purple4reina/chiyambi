Susan is a devoted mother in Katete, Zambia, facing an overwhelming
challenge: her child was born with a disability and requires constant
medication that she struggled to afford.

### The Process

Once Susan's project was fully funded, our team put together everything
she'd need to start a small business and set out for her home to deliver it
in person — as a surprise.

### The Results

Watch below as a member of our team arrives by bicycle with a basket full of
supplies for Susan. With a small business now underway, Susan is building a
steady source of income so she can consistently afford her child's
medication and provide for her family.
