Susan is a devoted mother in Katete, Zambia, facing an overwhelming
challenge: her child was born with a disability and requires constant
medication that she struggled to afford.

Thanks to the generosity of our supporters, Susan received the capital she
needed to start a small business. With a steady source of income, she is now
able to consistently provide her child's medication and work toward a more
stable, independent future for her family.
