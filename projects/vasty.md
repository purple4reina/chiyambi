Vasty is a resilient mother in Katete, Zambia, whose life was turned upside
down when her house tragically burned down, leaving her family with
nothing. Even after the community rallied to help rebuild her home, Vasty
and her children still lacked essentials like bedding, mattresses, and food.

### The Process

Once Vasty's story was fully funded, our team gathered bedding, mattresses,
and food, and traveled to her family's newly rebuilt home to deliver
everything in person.

### The Results

Watch below as our team arrives with everything Vasty's family needed to
start sleeping comfortably again. With these essentials in place, Vasty and
her children can now focus on healing and rebuilding their lives.
