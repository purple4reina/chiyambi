Who We Are

Chiyambi Community Foundation is a non-governmental organization dedicated to improving the lives of people in our community, Katete, Eastern Province, Zambia. Our mission is to uplift and empower vulnerable communities by providing essential resources and support. We are dedicated to ensuring access to clean and safe water, supporting vulnerable children and orphans with education, bursary and necessary supplies. We strive to alleviate poverty by proving food and other basic necessities, fostering a community where everyone has the opportunity to thrive and lead a fulfilling life.

We work by identifying micro projects that can have a big impact on the lives of people in our community. We are just getting started and we need your help to make a difference. If you would like to support our work, please consider making a donation.
