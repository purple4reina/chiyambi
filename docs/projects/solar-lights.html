Boosting Education in Katete with Solar Lights

Supporting Education
  • February 2025 The students' story was shared with our supporters
  • March 2025 The project was fully funded
  • May 2025 Solar lights were purchased, then lost in transit
  • December 2025 The lights reached the students

Students in Katete, Zambia, faced a harsh reality: frequent power cuts left them unable to study after dark, hurting their exam performance and educational goals.

The Process

Once the project was fully funded, our team purchased solar lights for the students of Katete. Along the way, the bus company transporting the lights lost the shipment. Our team kept supporters updated on the setback and worked to recover it before the lights could finally be delivered.

The Results

Watch below as the community first celebrates the news, then hear directly from a member of our team on the road to delivery, and finally see the students receive their solar lights — giving them a reliable, sustainable source of light to study by, regardless of the grid’s inconsistencies.
